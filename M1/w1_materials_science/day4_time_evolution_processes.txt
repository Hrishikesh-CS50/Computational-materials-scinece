Materials Science: Time Evolution Processes
Many Material Phenomena Evolve Step-by-Step

Examples:

Heating

Temperature changes over time.

Diffusion

Atoms migrate gradually.

Fatigue

Damage accumulates cycle by cycle.

Crystal Growth

Microstructure evolves incrementally.

Example: Heating Schedule
Time Step	Temperature
0	300
1	320
2	340
3	360

Generated by loop.

Example Code
for t in range(6):
    T = 300 + 20*t
    print("Step", t, "Temp", T)
Deeper Insight

Simulation is often:

Update state
Repeat many times
